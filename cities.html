Luanda

Luanda City

Sitting on the Western Coast of Africa, the port of Luanda is the capital and largest city in Angola. Founded by the Portuguese in 1575, the city has had a tumultuous history, finally achieving peace in 2002 after a long civil war. Now with a swelling population and surge of construction, the city is experiencing a rebirth.

Namibe

Namibe City

The Portuguese first arrived at Namibe in 1485. The city was named Moçâmedes and was the first district to be created in Southwestern Angola. In Namibe the sea, desert and savanna meet, providing breathtaking scenery and probably the best climate on the Angolan coast.
