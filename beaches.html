Angola

Luanda Beach

Luanda Beach, Luanda

The island of Luanda (or Cape Island) is a 7 km long tongue of land right in front of the bay of Luanda. This means a 7 km long beach just 2 minutes from the city center!

Cabo Ledo

Cabo Ledo Beach, Angola

Cabo ledo is a 1.5 hour drive from luanda and a great place to go for a day or two, expecially on the weekend.

Explore

Mussulo Beach

Mussulo Beach, Mussulo

Mussulo is a few km south to Luanda city center and is a 30km long tongue of land/sand. The water is even warmer than in rest of Angola and without waves, so it creates a (huge) lagoon-like environment in the sea.

Explore

Praia Morena

Praia Morena Beach, Benguela

This is the city's main beach, right near the center. On holidays it can get extremely crowded, but it's usually empty.

Explore

Porto Amboim

Porto Amboim Beach, Porto Amboim

Porto Amboim is halfway from Luanda to Benguela. We have never spent a whole day by the beach here, only made a stopover to have lunch on our way to Lobito/Benguela.

Ponta Do Ouro

Ponta Do Ouro, Mozambique

Ponta do Ouro is a breath of fresh ocean air, located just 10 kilometers by road from the South Africa border and butting up neatly to the deep-blue Indian Ocean.
